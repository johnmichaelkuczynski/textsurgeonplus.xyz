# 🔬 Text Surgeon

**The Serious Reader's AI — Extract, Compress, Rewrite, and Think Through Any Text**

---

## 🧩 Overview

Text Surgeon is a full-service text intelligence platform for scholars, writers, researchers, and serious readers who need more than a chatbot summary.

It extracts verbatim quotes, constructs formal arguments, compresses entire books into structured databases, rewrites manuscripts in any style, and produces coherent long-form analysis at scales that no standard AI tool can sustain.

Designed for **people who work with texts professionally**, it merges multi-provider LLM intelligence (OpenAI, Anthropic, Grok, DeepSeek, Perplexity) with a suite of precision tools that treat the source document — not a hallucinated paraphrase — as the ground truth.

---

## ✂️ What It Does

- **Clean Quotation List** — Pulls every significant verbatim quote from a text with full source attribution. No paraphrasing. No invention. Word-for-word extraction enforced at the prompt level.
- **Annotated Quotes** — Each extracted quote is paired with a commentary that explains its significance, context, and argumentative role.
- **Position Extractor** — Maps the author's positions holistically across the entire document, section by section, ranked by importance. Covers 5–15 positions per section. Configurable depth.
- **Arguments Finder** — Reconstructs formal arguments: premises, conclusions, and counterarguments. Configurable extraction depth from surface survey to exhaustive philosophical analysis.
- **Structured Summary** — Generates summaries at adjustable resolution — from a 2-paragraph overview of the whole work down to per-subsection breakdowns. Toggle between formal heading recognition and content-based thematic division.
- **Paragraph Compression** — Condenses dense prose to its logical core without losing argumentative structure.
- **15-Section Database** — Converts any text into a structured database across fifteen analytical categories: themes, arguments, rhetorical moves, key figures, conclusions, and more.
- **Tractatus Rewrite** — Converts any text into a sequence of numbered, one-line logical propositions in the style of Wittgenstein's *Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus*. Reveals the logical skeleton of any argument.
- **Tractatus Tree** — Multi-level abstraction view of the Tractatus output. Hierarchical numbered statements with depth-indented structure. Downloadable as .txt or .docx.
- **Full Document Rewrite** — Outline-guided section-by-section rewriting with custom instructions. Rewrites the full manuscript in any voice, register, or style you specify.
- **Stylometrics** — Deep stylometric profiling of an author's prose: vocabulary, sentence architecture, rhetorical patterns, verticality score, signal density. Compare two authors side by side.
- **Intelligence Meter** — Measures the signal-to-noise ratio of a text: how much of it is genuine intellectual content versus filler, hedge, or repetition.
- **Compare Intelligence** — Runs the intelligence analysis on two texts simultaneously and produces a head-to-head comparison.
- **Identify Major Views** — Extracts the main philosophical, ideological, or theoretical positions present in a text and maps their relationships.
- **Quote Finder** — Searches a text or your uploaded corpus for quotes by a specific author on a specific topic.
- **Text to Audio** — Converts manuscripts to MP3 or WAV using ElevenLabs neural voices. Single-voice narration or multi-speaker mode where you cast each voice and define who says what.

## ⚙️ Technical Features

- **Multi-Provider AI Backbone:** Switch between OpenAI (GPT-4o), Anthropic Claude, Grok (grok-3-latest), DeepSeek, and Perplexity — per analysis, per session. All providers run at temperature 0 for deterministic output.
- **Long Answer Mode:** Generates coherent responses up to 100,000 words using a two-phase skeleton-and-fill architecture with rolling memory summarization. No "pile of mini-essays" failure mode.
- **Pure Mode:** Constrains the AI to quote exclusively from texts you have uploaded. Extracts entities from your prompt, retrieves matching corpus chunks, and refuses to speculate beyond the source material.
- **Coherent Processing System:** For documents over 2,000 words, a state-tracked multi-chunk engine maintains logical consistency, thematic cohesion, and argumentative continuity across every section. Eight coherence modes: logical, scientific, thematic, instructional, motivational, mathematical, philosophical, and more.
- **All Day Mode:** Automated splitting and sequential processing of texts over 40,000 words. Chunks are processed with deliberate delays and results saved incrementally — so you can walk away and come back to a finished analysis.
- **Corpus Manager:** Build and maintain a private author corpus. Upload full texts; the system indexes them for Pure Mode retrieval and Quote Finder search.
- **SSE Streaming:** All major generation features stream token-by-token to the browser so you see results as they are produced, not after a long wait.
- **Export Everywhere:** Copy or download results at any time, including mid-generation. Word (.docx) export for Tractatus Tree. MP3/WAV download for audio.

## 💡 Core Idea

Most AI tools summarize. Text Surgeon **dissects**.

It treats every document as a structured object — an argument to be mapped, a voice to be profiled, a corpus to be mined. The output is not a chatbot's impression of your text. It is a systematic, verbatim-grounded, formally reasoned analysis produced at any scale you need.

No word limit. No hand-waving. No paraphrase passed off as a quote.
